About Me

Hi, I’m Benjie Bastez, a Marketing Specialist and Web Designer. Before entering the digital field, I spent 19 years in the construction industry—working as an electrician, machine operator, and eventually a foreman.

Those years taught me discipline, resilience, and the ability to build things with precision and purpose. I reached many milestones in that field, and I’m proud of everything I accomplished. But after nearly two decades, I felt it was time to pursue something new — something that would challenge me again and allow me to grow in a different direction.

Today, I’ve shifted into digital marketing and website creation because I believe I still have more to prove to myself. I want to use my skills, creativity, and work ethic to help business owners establish a strong online presence and present their brand in the best way possible.

I bring the same commitment and high standards from my construction background into every project I handle — building not with concrete and steel anymore, but with ideas, strategy, and design.

Let’s create something powerful for your business.

What I Offer

Lead Generation

Turn visitors into clients through targeted strategies

》

Lead Generation is the process of attracting and converting potential customers who are interested in your products or services.

It involves targeting the right audience, creating compelling offers, and using tools like landing pages, forms, and email campaigns to capture contact information.

Nurturing these leads with follow-ups and personalized messaging helps increase engagement and turn prospects into loyal clients.

By focusing on quality leads and smart strategies, businesses can grow consistently and improve their online conversions.

Facebook Ads

Reach the right audience and maximize ROI

》

Facebook Ads allow businesses to reach highly targeted audiences through paid campaigns on Facebook and Instagram.

By defining demographics, interests, and behaviors, you can show your ads to the people most likely to engage with your brand.

Effective campaigns use eye-catching creatives, compelling copy, and clear calls-to-action to drive clicks, leads, or sales.

Continuous monitoring and optimization help maximize your return on investment, ensuring your ads reach the right audience and generate measurable results.

Marketing Strategy

Smart plans to grow your business online

》

Marketing Strategy involves creating a comprehensive plan to promote your business effectively.

It includes identifying target audiences, analyzing competitors, creating messaging that resonates, and choosing the right channels to reach prospects.

A strong marketing strategy ensures your efforts are focused, measurable, and aligned with your business goals, helping you grow online consistently.

Web Design

Clean, conversion-focused landing pages & sites

》

Web Design focuses on creating websites that are visually appealing, easy to navigate, and optimized for user experience.

It involves layout planning, color schemes, typography, and responsive design to ensure the site looks great on all devices.

A well-designed website not only attracts visitors but also guides them to take desired actions, like making a purchase or booking a call.

By combining aesthetics with functionality, Web Design helps businesses establish credibility, engage users, and increase online conversions.

AI Automation

Streamline processes & save time using AI

》

AI Automation uses artificial intelligence tools and technologies to automate repetitive tasks and streamline business processes.

It can include automating email campaigns, data analysis, customer interactions, and workflow management.

By leveraging AI, businesses save time, reduce human error, and improve efficiency while focusing on higher-value tasks.

Implementing AI Automation allows companies to scale operations, optimize productivity, and enhance overall performance without adding extra manual work.
